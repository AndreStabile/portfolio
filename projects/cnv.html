CNV is a law firm that requested the services to build a website for its foundation.

The goal was that it was a mix of the seriousness expected of a law firm, but without the usual rigidity. Ideally, choose the middle way between seriousness and innovation.

The homepage needed to be serious enough and also show that CNV is not just any law firm.

It was also necessary to show the possible services to be hired and to immediately direct the contact with CNV.

Each of the service-specific pages needed to explain what it consists of, what step by step CNV should follow in execution, and additional information.

It is noteworthy that each of the service pages has a call-to-action for the customer to contact CNV.

As a frequent request from customers, a great focus was given to the CNV information section.

With a focus on the history of the company and its founders, we consider storytelling to bring confidence and peace of mind to the prospect.
