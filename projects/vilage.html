VILAGE Intellectual Property is a company specializing in trademark registration, patents and other related services.

With over 100 employees and over 25 offices across Brazil and around the world, VILAGE has gone so far as to need to completely redo its internal system.

There was also a need to improve the VILAGE site and ultimately rebuild the customer accessible system.

VILAGE's internal system

VILAGE's internal system takes care of customer acquisition, the monitoring of trademark registration processes, patents and other related services, as well as after-sales.

In addition, we still have employee control and internal company chat.

For each of the international agents, clients or other entities that have contact with VILAGE, we have a specific area to access informative, contact, address and bank account details.

Of course, if we have information to display, we also have their registration section.

As well as listing information with contextual searches.

It also has internal sections for access to various other functions and areas.

VILAGE's site

Rebuilding VILAGE's identity with its customers, we had the site redone.

For each section, we needed to explain the concepts to both expert clients who only needed one intermediary.                    as for inexperienced customers who needed a step-by-step from the start.

There are some variations in ways of explaining concepts, some involving infographics beyond texts.

In addition, all include a Frequently Asked Questions section.

Contact forms should not be missing for prospects to seek information about VILAGE services.

Demonstrating the reach of VILAGE, the Offices screen shows more information and contact information about each of the company's 30 offices.

To increase credibility, despite 32 years of company, we decided to make the Customer Testimonials page.

As a way to increase the company's reach and representativeness to society, we have a news section.

News is collected and curated from various points related to trademarks and patents.

infoVILAGE

As a way to give customers direct access to all their processes in various countries, infoVILAGE was planned.

Providing a way for the client to observe, track and make decisions about their intellectual properties.

Offering contextual searches aligned to users' purposes.

Dashboards with concise, direct and relevant information at a glance.

Also needed to be the need to upload and download files within the system.

Forms containing a variety of ways to enter data, including even selecting specific colors for search tags.

Also, a special section called "Danger Zone" where sensitive actions like deleting important data are contained separately.

It is very important for clients to be able to see the history of court decisions about their lawsuits.

Simple and complex reports so that the customer has a complete sense of the progress of their processes, and can print if they want to have in physical version.
